# Templates

## 1. Deal Analysis

### Executive Summary
- What happened
- Key takeaway
- Core value driver

### Context
- Sector and company positioning

### Strategic Rationale
- Why the deal happened
- What each party gains

### Value Creation
- Revenue, cost, strategic levers

### Economics
- Valuation (if available)
- Pricing logic

### Risks
- Key risks specific to the deal

### Scenario Analysis
- Multiple forward paths
- Key drivers

### Conclusion
- Clear, grounded stance

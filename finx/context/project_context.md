# Project Context

## Objective

The objective of this project is to build a high-signal, public-facing research layer that demonstrates the ability to perform institutional-quality financial and strategic analysis.

This is designed specifically for:
- Investment banking recruitment
- Private equity recruitment

The output must signal that the author can:
- Analyze transactions independently
- Understand value creation mechanisms
- Interpret financial and strategic implications of deals
- Produce structured, decision-relevant output

---

## What This Is

- A curated research layer
- A demonstration of analytical capability
- A system for consistent, high-quality deal and investment analysis

---

## What This Is Not

- Not a blog
- Not opinion-driven content
- Not educational or explanatory writing for general audiences
- Not academic coursework-style output

---

## Core Analytical Philosophy

All analysis must answer:

1. Why did this happen?
2. How does this create value?
3. What are the key economic drivers?
4. What could happen next under different scenarios?

---

## Output Philosophy

- Non-opinionated, fact-based analysis
- Scenario-driven, not prediction-driven
- Structured and concise
- Focused on decision-relevant insights

---

## Success Criteria

A piece is considered successful if:
- It resembles internal output from an investment bank or private equity firm
- It is defensible under scrutiny
- It avoids generic explanations and unnecessary detail
- It clearly demonstrates both technical and strategic understanding

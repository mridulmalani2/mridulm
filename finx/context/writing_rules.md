# Writing Rules

## Sentence Structure

- Prefer short to medium-length sentences
- Avoid overly complex phrasing
- Maintain clarity and precision

---

## Style by Content Type

### Deal Analysis
- Structured
- Direct
- Minimal narrative

### Investment Memo
- Slightly more narrative
- Still concise
- Decision-oriented

### Sector Analysis
- More explanatory
- Still analytical

### Short Notes
- Sharp
- Compressed

---

## Language

- Use precise financial terminology
- Avoid informal language
- Avoid exaggerated wording

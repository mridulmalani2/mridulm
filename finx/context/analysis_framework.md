# Analysis Framework

All work must follow this analytical spine:

Macro → Sector → Company → Deal → Value Creation → Outcomes

---

## 1. Macro Context (when relevant)
- Economic environment
- Capital availability
- Industry-wide shifts

---

## 2. Sector Context
- Market structure
- Growth drivers
- Competitive dynamics
- Where value accrues

---

## 3. Company Context
- Business model
- Revenue streams
- Strategic positioning
- Financial profile (high-level)

---

## 4. Deal Overview
- Transaction structure
- Parties involved
- Timing and context

---

## 5. Value Creation

This is the core of every analysis.

Must include:
- Revenue growth levers
- Cost efficiencies
- Strategic synergies
- Capital structure optimization (if relevant)

---

## 6. Economics / Valuation

Where relevant:
- Valuation method (DCF, comps, precedent transactions)
- Key assumptions
- Pricing sanity check

---

## 7. Risks

- Execution risk
- Market risk
- Financial risk
- Structural risks

Must be specific and grounded.

---

## 8. Scenario Analysis

Do not provide a single prediction.

Provide:
- Multiple plausible outcomes
- Key drivers for each
- Sensitivities where relevant

---

## 9. Conclusion

- Clear, defensible stance
- Based on analysis, not opinion
- No speculative language

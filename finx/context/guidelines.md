# Guidelines

## Hard Constraints

- No fluff
- No generic explanations
- No long introductions
- No em dashes
- No academic-style writing
- No “this article explores” phrasing

---

## Writing Rules

- Every sentence must add value
- Avoid repetition
- Avoid filler transitions
- Keep language precise and technical

---

## Analytical Rules

- No unsupported claims
- No speculation without structure
- No single-outcome predictions
- Always include scenario-based thinking

---

## Tone

- Neutral
- Analytical
- Professional

---

## Structure

- Executive summary is mandatory
- Sections must be logically ordered
- No forced structure, but consistent flow

---

## Output Standard

Each piece must:
- Be defensible under scrutiny
- Reflect real-world finance work
- Avoid looking like student output

# Claude Instructions

This workspace is designed to produce institutional-quality financial analysis for investment banking and private equity contexts.

All outputs must strictly follow the rules and frameworks defined in the following files:

- /context/project_context.md
- /context/analysis_framework.md
- /context/guidelines.md
- /context/writing_rules.md
- /templates/templates.md
- /system/memory.md
- /system/quality_checklist.md

---

## Core Directives

- Prioritize analytical rigor over completeness
- Focus only on decision-relevant information
- Avoid all generic explanations
- Maintain neutral, non-opinionated tone
- Use scenario-based analysis instead of predictions

---

## Output Requirements

Every output must:

1. Include an executive summary at the top
2. Clearly explain value creation
3. Include risks that are specific and grounded
4. Provide multiple forward scenarios where relevant
5. End with a clear, defensible conclusion

---

## Prohibited Behavior

- No fluff or filler
- No academic-style explanations
- No “this article explores” phrasing
- No em dashes
- No unsupported claims

---

## Execution

- Use templates as structural guidance, not rigid constraints
- Maintain consistency across outputs
- Reference memory only when relevant
- Optimize for clarity and precision

---

## Standard

Outputs must resemble internal work from investment banks or private equity firms.
